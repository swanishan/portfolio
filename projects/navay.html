The new ways of life...


A platform that aids immigrants, expats, travelers, better assimilate into a new world with tips, forum, and chats.

Jump to Prototype

Challenge

An immigrant, expatriate, or an international student require assistance in learning the new ways of life in the new country they are moving to. Navigation through the first few days, settling in, or facing trouble in completing even the minuscule tasks can be a difficult and an overbearing experience.

The ways and culture of any country are hundreds, if not thousands of years old, and are extremely layered. Comprehending them and fully adjusting to them can take years. In the fast-paced modern world the actors mentioned above would already have so much else to do, and it could be adjusting for them even more intimidating.

Solution

The solution to this is an app that collects data, organizes it, and displays the information in an efficient way. The app shall focus on even the most minute aspects and tasks of life and share them with the user.

This project has 2 user groups:

  • Primary — International Students, Expatriates, Immigrants
  • Secondary — Foreign/Semester Exchange Students, Expatriates' Families, Tourists

User Interviews with IDEO methods (thought scribbling and card sort) gave serious insights into the experiences and challenges faced by the interviewees while changing countries.

Comprehensive: The culture and the ways of a country are extremely layered and go very deep into the daily life of its citizens. The app needs to analyze all the domains of everyday life, up to the most minuscule details and help users acclimatize.

Non-judgemental: Moving to a new country is a heavy decision and a difficult process. The comprehensive change can be overbearing and the troubles of the immigrants, whether big or small, should not be judged, but be helped with.

Supportive: The purpose of this app to provide support to the users in every way possible. The app needs to be extensive and try to help the users in all possible ways.

Robust, Smart, & Dynamic: The app needs to be smart in order to scour the data that is available on the internet, be dynamic in order to change with time and new data that is collected, and robust in order to handle all the other two.

Create a Community: The users of this are new in the country they are in and have negligible contacts. The app needs to create a sense of community so that the users feel comfortable when interacting with the app and its other users.
