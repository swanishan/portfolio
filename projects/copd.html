COPD Treatment

An AstraZeneca Mexico website for doctors to know about COPD, and AZMED medicines and therapies. The website has blogs and tests for patients and doctors to check if they suffer from COPD. COPD = EPOC en Español

Live Website Jump to Prototype

The Project

The aim of this project was to design a website that educates about COPD, its symptoms, and how serious the conditions are in Mexico.

The website talks in detail about the Trixeo and Breztri Aerosphere drugs and the treatment options and therapies that are available to the patients. It also also provides information about multiple studies in detail along with blogs to expand on how the medication works.

There is also a test available to users to check how likely they are to have COPD.

Project Success

The website was developed by respective professionals at TCS and AZ Mexico. It is live now and can be viewed here.
